Protect Your Computer : Installing Anti-Virus Software

SmadAV 2015

▩ By: Elyes Khechine
▩ March 27 2015

4 main functions SmadAV:

1) additional protection for your computer, 100% compatible with other antivirus!

Most antivirus can not be installed alongside other antivirus, it is because the antivirus is designed for primary protection on your computer. In contrast to SmadAV, SmadAV is the type of antivirus in-design as an additional protection, so that 100% compatible and can work well in spite of the other antivirus in your computer, in this case SmadAV serves as a second line of defense. SmadAV has its own way (behavior, heuristic, and whitelisting) to detect and clean viruses that will further enhance security on the computer. Due to resource usage SmadAV very small, it will not add weight SmadAV performance of your computer in use. So, with a fusion between SmadAV and antivirus protection that is installed on your computer will further strengthen the defense of your computer from virus infection.

2) Best USB Antivirus (Total Protection USB stick)

USB stick is one of the largest media spread of the virus in Indonesia. SmadAV have special technology for total prevention of virus that spreads via USB stick. SmadAV mission is 100% no longer infectious virus from pendrive. SmadAV have enough signatures virus that infects the flash, and has the special ability to detect new viruses in the flash although not in the database SmadAV. Not only prevention, SmadAV also able to clean a virus that infects and restore virus hidden files in the USB stick

3) Best for offline use (no need to update too often)

SmadAV very well be used for computers that are rarely or even not connected to the internet. SmadAV do not need to update this as often as other antivirus that usually do updates per week and even per day. SmadAV usually do updates only once a month (monthly). SmadAV not overly dependent on the signature / virus database, but rely more on behavior detection techniques, heuristics, and whitelisting.

4) Cleaner and tools to clean the virus

SmadAV also able to clear the virus that has infected your computer and repair registry altered by the virus. Other antivirus usually not done cleaning the registry so that your computer has not returned to normal after cleaning the antivirus. Many supporting tools that are included in SmadAV as a weapon for cleaning the virus. Note: Not all types of viruses can be cleaned SmadAV, SmadAV is still not able to clear the virus type or types of rootkits penginfeksi program (for example: Ramnit virus, Sality, Alman, Virut, etc.) Because this strain has destroyed most of your program files . Our current focus is due to the type of virus clearance in addition to the two types (eg virus WormShortcut, Cervical, MSO, Brontok, etc.).

5) What are the advantages SmadAV Pro compared SmadAV Free?

SmadAV Pro has many additional features that do not exist in SmadAV Free, the following are additional features that you would get at SmadAV Pro: Automatic Update Online, Scanning Faster, Exception List, Maximize / Resize, Change Color Theme , Admin Password, Profit and Use Permit. You must be a member to get SmadAV Pro. Note: SmadAV Free & Pro has the same detection capability. The difference is only in the auto-update feature and other additional features.
